Who are we?

As native Indonesians who grew up with a great appreciation for the cuisine of our culture, we developed a passion for cooking years ago. It was not until recently that we had the opportunity to truly explore what it would be like to cook and prepare this food and share it with those who are close to us. Over time, word of mouth spread, and we upgraded our small business so that we could provide more orders to those who wish to enjoy the wonderful cuisine of South Asia!

We strive for the most authentic and true-to-culture product we can! However, if anything is not to your liking, we welcome any feedback or suggestion!
